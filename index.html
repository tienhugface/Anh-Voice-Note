Voice Note

Capture ideas, memories, conversations, and daily events with your voice. Voice Note combines audio recording, speech-to-text transcription, photos, documents, and location information into a single easy-to-use note.

Designed with simplicity in mind, Voice Note makes it easy to record first and organize later.

Features

🎤 Voice Recording

* Record high-quality audio notes
* Multiple recordings per note
* Simple playback controls

📝 Automatic Transcription

* Convert speech into text automatically
* Review recordings alongside transcripts
* Edit transcripts while keeping the original recording

📷 Photo Attachments

* Take photos directly from the camera
* Select images from your photo library
* Keep visual memories together with recordings

📄 Document Scanning

* Scan paper documents using your device camera
* Store documents alongside notes and recordings

📍 Location Awareness

* Save location information with notes
* Easily remember where a recording was created

🔍 Search

* Search notes by transcript text
* Quickly find important recordings

💾 Backup & Restore

* Create complete backups of your data
* Restore notes when changing devices
* Share backups using Files or AirDrop

Designed for Simplicity

Voice Note features large controls and a clean interface that make recording and reviewing notes quick and straightforward.

Privacy

Your recordings, transcripts, photos, and documents are stored on your device. You remain in control of your data.
